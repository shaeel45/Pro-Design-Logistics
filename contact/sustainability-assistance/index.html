Consumer behavior, government regulations and investor demands have heightened the expectation for shippers to reduce emissions. But to set reduction goals, companies first need to understand their carbon output. Because of the complexity, most companies aren’t measuring transportation emissions or investing in reduction at all. And for those that are measuring, they’re likely coming up with their own calculations and have little to no benchmarking data to evaluate where they stand or judge their progress over time.

For decades, we’ve helped companies improve how they transport their goods—constantly looking to decrease inventory, reduce waste, rationalize location, consolidate freight. It’s what we do as one of the largest logistics providers around the world. We help increase transportation efficiency, which in turn lowers greenhouse gas emissions.

Our technology and supply chain experts can help you discover efficiency opportunities, develop a plan, and create a road map for change across your global supply chain.
